ABOUT US:
Exbooks is an organization that streamlines the recycling of used books. We strive to connect people who might be in need of books, that are usually really expensive, to people who offer them at a much cheaper rate.
Our Mission:
"To increase and improve the way the community reuses books by providing them at the cheapest rates possible"
Our Vision:
A better world where people reuse books more than they don't.
